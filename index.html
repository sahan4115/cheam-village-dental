Cheam Village is run by Dr Krishan & Dr Kaajal Patel, a husband and wife team who built the kind of practice they'd want for their own family.

One small team, looking after yours. You'll see the same familiar faces each visit, with unhurried appointments and treatment explained in plain, everyday language, never jargon.

From children's first check-ups to teeth straightening and complete smile makeovers, the whole family is welcome, and looked after with the same care, year after year.
